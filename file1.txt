PB&J

Ingredients
- Peanut butter
- Jelly
- Bread

Steps
1. Gather the ingredients, a butter knife, and a paper towel.
2. Place two slices of bread on the paper towel.
3. Open the jelly and use the knife to gather some and spread it on one of the slices of bread.
4. Rinse the knife.
5. Open the peanut butter and use the knife to gather some and spread it on the slice of bread without jelly.
6. Place the slice of bread with the peanut butter on top of the slice with jelly with the peanut butter side down.
